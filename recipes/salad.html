How to Prepare Ghanaian Salad

A typical salad made in Ghana.

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 as needed Heinz Salad Cream
  • 1 can Heinz Baked Beans
  • 1 package Romaine Salad Mix

Instructions

  1. Boil eggs
  2. Slice tomatoes
  3. Slice onions
  4. Start the salad once eggs are cooked by slicing the eggs
  5. Mix the romaine salad mix with all sliced ingredients
  6. Add baked beans
  7. Top with salad cream
